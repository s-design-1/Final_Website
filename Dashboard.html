"My hobby is writing my feelings on paper"

Because for me, writing has always been a way for me to express and understand my emotions. Because there is something profoundly therapeutic about putting my feelings on paper, whether it’s a moment of joy, frustration, confusion, or sadness, the act of writing helps me process these emotions in a way that talking or thinking alone cannot. I find that when I write, I can release pent-up feelings, organize my thoughts, and gain clarity on what’s going on in my mind.
